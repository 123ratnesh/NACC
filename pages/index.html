About GGITS

Introduction: Gyan Ganga Institute of Technology and Sciences is a professional institution in Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh, India. It was founded in 2003 and offers bachelor's and master's degrees, and diplomas in engineering, pharmacy, and management. It is affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi Praudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya and Institute has ME, EC, EE and CSE Accredited by NBA (National Board of Accreditation) of the All India Council for Technical Education. GGITS is part of the Gyan Ganga Group of Institutions
